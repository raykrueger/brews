Zhukov's Revenge

Based on Czar's Revenge from Brewing Classic Styles. You'll need a really big ass mash tun to pull this off. We're talking about 24lbs of grain.

OG: 1.092

Mash Ingredients

Maris Otter20 lbs
Black Barley1.5 lbs
Special B1 lb
German Caramunich III.5 lbs
Pale Chocolate Malt.5 lbs
Chocolate Malt.5 lbs

Boil Additions

Magnum13%1 oz60 min
Kent Goldings4.5%2 oz10 min
Kent Goldings4.5%1 oz1 min

Yeast

Wyeast 1056 American Ale

Instructions

Mash at 154°

Carbonate to 2.0-2.5 volumes

Brew Day 09-26-2010

The Mash

I totally screwed up the mash on this. I didn't use a calculator to figure out the mash and sparge water volumes and temperatures. My dough-in temp was way low. I kept adding hot water till I couldn't add any more. Once it was too full I started draining wort, boiling it, and putting it back in until I got my temp. Then I let it mash. A hacked up decoction mash almost.

Gravity

OG: 1.092, FG: 1.020

Bottled 10-10-2010

Primed using 3.4 oz of corn sugar

Tasting Notes

  • 11-25-2010 After almost 2 months in the bottle this beer is really coming in great. Huge roast flavor with just the right mouthfeel. Not syrupy sweet and not dry, right down the middle.
