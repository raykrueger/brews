Homebrew Notes

Easy Ryeder

Ryed up American Pale Ale with a touch of rye whiskey added to the boil. Half of this batch will be aged with medium-toast American oak soaked in Old Overholt Straight Rye Whiskey. The oak will get the rye whiskey soak for one week and then get added to the fermenter for a few weeks.

OG: 1.042

Mash Ingredients

2-Row Malt4 lbs
Pale Ale Malt4 lbs
Rye Malt3 lbs
Caramel Vienne10 oz
Caramel 205 oz

Boil Additions

Magnum13.7%0.75 oz60 min
Centenial10%0.75 oz15 min
Cascade5.4%0.755 min
Old Overholt Rye Whiskey 2 oz 30 min 40%

Instructions

Mash at 154°

The Mash

Really well executed. Temp came in a little low, had to top it off with some boiling water.

The Boil

Straight 60 minute boil here. Dosed the brew with a bit of whiskey on a whim, we'll see how that turns out.
