Two-Hand Reel

Another recipe out of Brewing Classic Styles. This time it is the Irish red ale recipe, "Ruabeior".

OG: 1.060

Mash Ingredients

British Pale Ale Malt11.25 lbs
Caramel 406 0z
Caramel 1206 oz
Roasted Barley6 oz

Boil Additions

Kent Goldings4.5%1.25 oz60 min

Yeast

Wyeast 1084 Irish Ale

Instructions

Mash at 154°

Carbonate to 2.5 volumes

Brew Day 11-14-2010

The Mash

All in all this went well. Unfortunately I didn't start the sparge water early enough which left the mash going into a 90 minute range. Also, the sparge water (due to the delay) wasn't hot enough. We hit just around 168° in the mash tun when it should have been 170°.

The Boil

Went with a 90 minute boil to try and bring up the caramelization and bring out a good red hue. We'll see how that works out.

Gravity

OG: Unknown (drunk), FG: 1.010

Bottled 12-05-2010

Using 4oz Corn Sugar to 2.5 volumes of co2
